    Who We Are

    We are a small team of passionate growers transforming city spaces into thriving food gardens. Local Urban Farmers began with a simple goal—bring fresh, local produce to our neighbors and build a greener community.

    What We Do

    • 🌿 Host gardening & composting workshops
    • 🌽 Grow seasonal fruits & vegetables
    • 🌎 Promote urban sustainability
    • 🤝 Collaborate with restaurants & schools
